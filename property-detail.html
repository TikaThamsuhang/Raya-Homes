About This Home

97 Schnoor Road

Beautifully updated 1,800 sq. ft. four-bedroom Colonial set on a private 3.3-acre lot with an oversized two-car garage. This move-in-ready home features an eat-in kitchen with cherry cabinetry, granite countertops, tile flooring, and new appliances, opening to a comfortable sitting area with a fireplace. Recent improvements include new hardwood floors, updated bathrooms, and a modern heating system.

The outdoor space is a private oasis with a stone patio, mature trees, and plenty of room for a pool. Located in a quiet cul-de-sac neighborhood, just minutes from town center and schools.

Additional features include a finished basement with potential for a home office or gym, a newer roof (2020), and a generator hookup. The property is conveniently situated near major highways for easy commuting, while still offering the tranquility of a countryside setting. Don't miss the opportunity to own this well-maintained home in a sought-after community.
